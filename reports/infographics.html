AI to Combat Stunting

An Analysis of Childhood Stunting in Mamberamo Raya, Papua

Predictive Model Accuracy

88.67%

An XGBoost machine learning model was trained to accurately classify children as "Stunting" or "Not Stunting" based on key demographic and environmental factors, providing a powerful tool for early identification.

Dataset Overview

The model was developed using 607 anonymized records from the Ministry of Health, Indonesia, providing a robust foundation for analysis.

Out of 122 test cases, the model demonstrated high reliability in identifying both stunting and non-stunting instances, proving its value for field application.

What are the Key Drivers of Stunting?

Interpretable AI (SHAP) was used to identify which factors have the most significant impact on the model's predictions. The results clearly point to two primary drivers.

2. Difficult Access to Services
